# 🍫 Choco de Luxe Sales Insight Dashboard

## 📊 Overview

The **Choco de Luxe Sales Insight Dashboard** is an interactive Tableau Business Intelligence solution designed to provide a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, product demand, sales channel effectiveness, and regional performance. The dashboard empowers business leaders with actionable insights to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), evaluate sales trends, identify top-performing products and sales representatives, and make informed strategic decisions.

This project demonstrates practical expertise in Tableau, Business Intelligence, Sales Analytics, KPI development, and data storytelling through interactive and visually engaging dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

In today's competitive retail and consumer goods industry, organizations need accurate and timely insights to maximize revenue, improve profitability, and optimize sales performance. Without centralized reporting, it becomes difficult to identify top-performing products, monitor regional sales, evaluate sales channels, and measure employee performance.

This dashboard was developed to answer key business questions such as:

* Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
* Which sales channels are the most effective?
* How is revenue trending over time?
* Which countries contribute the highest sales?
* Which sales representatives consistently outperform their targets?
* How does product demand differ between Organic and Non-Organic products?
* What opportunities exist to improve profitability and reduce cancelled sales?

## 📊 Key Insights

📈 The business generated **€5.13M** in total revenue.

💰 Total profit reached **€2.36M**, resulting in a strong **46% profit margin**.

🍫 **Dark Chocolate** emerged as the highest-performing product category, contributing over **26%** of total profitability.

🌐 The **Website** sales channel generated the highest customer engagement and sales performance.

🌍 **Italy** recorded the highest number of completed sales among all countries analyzed.

👤 Several sales representatives exceeded **100%** of their performance targets, demonstrating exceptional sales effectiveness.

📦 Customer demand varied significantly between Organic and Non-Organic products across different sales channels, highlighting opportunities for targeted marketing and inventory optimization.
